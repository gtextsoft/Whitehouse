Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are Whitehouse lands verified?

Yes. We provide verifiable documents and encourage independent checks. Every plot comes with comprehensive documentation including survey plans and title deeds.

Q: Can I pay in installments?

Yes. Each estate comes with flexible payment plans including outright purchase and installment options (typically 3, 6, and 12 months).

Q: Do you offer inspection?

Yes. We offer both physical and virtual inspections which can be booked through our contact page. Inspections are completely free.

Q: When do I get allocation?

Allocation follows your payment milestone as stated in your contract. Our team will guide you through the entire process.

Q: What documents will I receive?

You will receive: Survey Plan, Deed of Assignment, Contract of Sale, Receipt, and other location-specific approvals.

Q: Can I resell my plot?

Yes. After full payment and allocation, you have complete ownership rights including the ability to resell, develop, or hold for appreciation.

Q: Which location is best for me?

It depends on your budget and investment goals. Contact our advisors for personalized recommendations based on your needs.
